Chicken Salad

Description : Grilled chicken breast with multiple kinds of vegetables including lettuce, avocado, tomato, onion, cucumber and etc. You are welcome to add your choice of salad dressing. I recommend balsamic vinegar with some olive oil. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • a whole chicken breast
  • lettuce
  • tomato
  • onion
  • avocado
  • cucumber
  • balsamic vinegar
  • olive oil(optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare all the vegetables. Clean them first and chop them into bite size.
  2. Leave chopped vegetables aside. Grill a piece of chicken breast. Make sure to cook it throughly.
  3. After chicken breast is fully cooked, chop them into bite size as well.
  4. Place vegetable as base layer first. On top of that, place chicken breast.
  5. Lastly, put balsamic vinegar and olive oil as much as you want to.
  6. All set! Enjoy!
